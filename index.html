Residential & Commercial Electrical Services

Switchboard Upgrades Adelaide: Old switchboards are a safety risk. If your home still has ceramic fuses or lacks RCD protection, you're gambling with electrical safety. I upgrade switchboards to modern standards with RCBO protection, proper circuit labeling, and surge protection. All upgrades meet current AS/NZS 3000 wiring standards and come with electrical safety certificates for insurance and real estate compliance.

Emergency Electrician Available 24/7: Power outages don't wait for business hours. Sparking outlets, tripping circuits, smoke from switchboards-these are emergencies that need immediate attention. I'm available 24/7 for genuine electrical emergencies across Adelaide metro. Call 0410 414 969 and I'll talk you through what's safe while I'm on my way.

EV Charger Installation: Got an electric vehicle? I install Level 2 EV chargers (7kW-22kW) with proper electrical certification. Whether it's a Tesla Wall Connector, Zappi, or universal Type 2 socket, I'll assess your existing electrical capacity, upgrade if needed, and install your charger safely. Fully compliant with EV charger installation standards.

LED Lighting & Ceiling Fans: Upgrading to LED downlights saves energy and improves lighting quality. I install dimmable LED downlights, sensor lights, ceiling fans, and outdoor security lighting. All installations include proper insulation clearances (IC-F rated), fire-rated covers where required, and clean ceiling finishes-no messy cutouts or dodgy trim rings.

Commercial Electrical Work: I also handle commercial jobs-shop fit-outs, office electrical upgrades, three-phase installations, data cabling, and emergency lighting compliance. Licensed for commercial work up to small-medium premises. If you're a property manager, strata company, or business owner needing electrical work, I provide proper electrical safety compliance certificates and defect reporting.
