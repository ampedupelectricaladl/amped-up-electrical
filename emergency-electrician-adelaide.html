Emergency Callout FAQs

How fast can you get here?

Depends on location and time of day. I'll give you an accurate ETA when you call. Most emergency callouts within Adelaide metro responded to quickly.

Do you charge extra for after-hours?

Emergency callout fee applies 24/7. If you proceed with the repair, callout fee is included in the total job cost. Clear pricing before any work starts.

What if you can't fix it tonight?

I'll make it safe (isolate faulty circuits, temporary repairs) so you have power to essential areas. Schedule full repair for next day if needed.

Do you warranty emergency work?

Yes. All electrical work is warranted and AS/NZS 3000 compliant, emergency or not.

Should I turn off the main switch?

If you smell burning or see sparks, YES - turn off the main switch immediately. If you're unsure, call me first and I'll talk you through it safely.
